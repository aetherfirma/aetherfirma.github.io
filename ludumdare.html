Ludum Dare

Ludum Dare is a thrice annual 48 hour game making competition, where thousands of people around the world compete to build a game to a theme announced at the start of the competition.

I've entered the competition six times so far, and completed it three times.

Prison Break (LD33)

Prison Break is a 3D twitch space dogfighting game, and an exercise in making sure you leave some time to follow the theme. (Entry Page)

You Only Dare Thrice (LD32)

A procedurally generated spy thriller, you play as a spy tracking down the stolen components of a superweapon. The dialogue system was neat, even if the story and gameplay needed work. Whilst I ran out of time to put in proper buildings, the placeholders served the gameplay well regardless. (Entry Page)

Inquisition (LD28)

Your daughter has been kidnapped by the inquisition to buy your compliance. You have 28 days to either submit yourself to them, or flee the country. Can you save your daughter? Arguably my favourite of my entries and the one that works the best. I'd quite like to go back and revisit this at some point, turn it into more of a proper game. (Entry Page)
